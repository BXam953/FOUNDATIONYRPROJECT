Societies and Clubs

The Students' Union has over 400 societies, so it's easy to find something that interests you.There are numerous ways to get involved or try something new, ranging from meditation to hiking to musical theater.There are also societies for each country in which international students can participate and meet people from their home country. And if you can't find anything that interests you, you can form your own society.

Some other examples include:

  • 180 Degrees Consulting Manchester
  • 3D Printing Society
  • 5AM Society
  • 90s Hip Hop Society
  • A Cappella and Beatboxing Society (UMACABS)
  • Access All Areas Projects/Volunteers
  • Acclivus Finance Society
  • Accounting Society Manchester

Visit the Students' Union website to learn about some of the societies you can join and how to get involved
