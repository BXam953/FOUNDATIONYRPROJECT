Your Accomodation

Bisham Paracha, 20th April 2022

When looking for a place to live in Manchester or elsewhere, your are going to be met with two types of accommodation.

Student Accommodation is the most common choice amongst the students of UoM. It is an accommodation built especially for students, some of which may contain facilities such as cinemas, lounge areas, study rooms… etc. When it comes to the type of room you wish to stay in, this type of accommodations offer the following options: Studio Apartments, Private Apartments, Shared Apartments, En-Suits… etc.

Here are some of the recommended student accommodations:

  • UoM Student Accomodation
    • The University of Manchester offers a variety of rooms for their students in over 20 halls around the campus
  • Hello Student
    • Hello Student is ideal if you are looking for fancy student accommodation only moments away from the University of Manchester.
  • Vita Student
      • Are you looking for a luxury student accommodation not further than 5 minutes walk from the university? Your best option here is Vita Student!
  • iQ Student Accomodation
      • Want to live near either the south or the north campus of UoM? We would recommend you iQ student accommodation, as they have around 7 buildings around the University of Manchester alone! They also offers variety of room options, 1-3 bedroom apartments/ duplexes, studios, en-suits and much more.
  • Unite Students
      • There is a reason that rooms at Unite Students become sold out the second the booking opens, its perfect location on Oxford St., and the fascinating range of rooms and facilities, and let us not forget to mention the Free Living Essentials and 24/7 security and assistance that Unite Students provide to their residents.
